## 1. Returns & Refund Policy
**Slug:** `returns-policy`

### Our Commitment to You
At ShoeStar, we stand behind the quality of our products. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

### Return Window
You have **7 calendar days** to request a return for an item from the date you received it. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, in the same condition that you received it, and in its original packaging.

### Ineligible Items
For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:
- Socks
- Insoles
- Limited Edition releases (as specified at time of purchase)

### How to Initiate a Return
1. Log in to your ShoeStar account.
2. Navigate to **My Orders**.
3. Select the delivered order you wish to return.
4. Click on **Request Return** (available only within 7 days of delivery).
5. Once approved, you will receive instructions on how to send the item back to us.

### Refunds
Once we receive and inspect your item, we will notify you of the status of your refund.
- If approved, we will initiate a refund to your **ShoeStar Wallet**.
- Wallet balances can be used for future purchases or withdrawn (minimum KSh 1,000).
- If the original payment was made via **Coins**, the refund will be credited back to your Coins balance.

### Shipping Costs for Returns
Customers are responsible for paying their own shipping costs for returning items. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If the item is defective or we sent the wrong product, ShoeStar will cover the return shipping costs.

### Exchanges
We currently process exchanges by first returning the original item and then placing a new order. For administrative exchanges, a new delivery fee will apply.
